About Our School

Welcome to Sunshine Public School

Established in 1995, Sunshine Public School has been a beacon of quality education in our community. Our mission is to provide a nurturing environment that fosters academic excellence, character development, and lifelong learning.

With state-of-the-art facilities and a dedicated faculty, we prepare students to meet the challenges of an ever-changing world while maintaining strong moral values and social responsibility.
