About Our School

Established in 1995, Sunshine Public School has been providing quality education to students in a nurturing environment. Our mission is to develop well-rounded, confident and responsible individuals who aspire to achieve their full potential.

School Facilities:

  • Smart classrooms with digital boards
  • Well-equipped science and computer labs
  • Library with 10,000+ books
  • Sports facilities including basketball court and swimming pool
  • Music and art rooms
  • Safe and secure campus with CCTV surveillance

Academic Calendar:

We conduct two major examinations each year:

  1. Mid-Term Examination (July)
  2. Annual Examination (December)

Results are typically published within 30 days after each examination.
